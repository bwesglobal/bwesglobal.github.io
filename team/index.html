Our Journey

From Dairy farming to commodity trading to cargo heating management service to a full fledged Marine Software as service provider today- The journey of our company has been more than just challenging and exciting. Blue Water Trade Winds Pvt. Ltd, as the name may suggest, was incorporated by fellow mariners in year 2006 for carrying out large scale commodity trading. It took us almost two years to realize that Trade Winds only assist a vessel at sea but not an organization in Dehradun (India) at the foothills of Himalayas, thousands of miles away from sea!

Almost fragmented but still determined to do business, it was in year 2008 that our innovative product & service for optimizing cargo-heating operations onboard was highly acclaimed by one of the leading tanker owner. Within a short span of 3 years, majority of tanker owners and operators, including oil majors, started hiring our cost effective Cargo Heating Management (CHM) services.

Upon realization of our core strengths in marine domain, Blue Water undertook many diversified marine projects until 2015. The services included Dry Docking of vessels, PMS data transition & training onboard, Electronic Oil Record Book, Port Captaincy and Loss Control and many more.

In the fall of year 2016, we embarked on yet another project that was initially aimed at harnessing savings arising out of Virtual Arrival exercise. Soon we learnt that, industry required a much extensive system for optimizing and managing its fleet. A rather ambitious project - Bluewater Optimum Speed Service (BOSS) was then kicked off later in the same year. BOSS today, having eighteen innovative modules, is the result of our core marine competency coupled with latest AI and ML technology.
