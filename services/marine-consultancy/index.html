Marine Consultancy

Blue Water provides a wide range of consultancy services globally on demand from surveys/inspection to the development of a diverse range of customized applications. We offer a complete range of information services essential in managing a wide range of marine industry.

  • Dry-dock project management: We have a dedicated team to oversee and manage a comprehensive range of marine repair and maintenance programs for vessels and fleets of all sizes. Our expert team ensures that all repairs and maintenance are carried out professionally and efficiently to the highest industry standards.
  • Training and onboard deployment of planned maintenance system: Blue Water has a team of PMS engineers specializing in implementing fleet maintenance solutions, both onboard as well as ashore locations. We assist in the smooth transition from existing maintaining system to the latest PMS.
  • Marine Customized Application Development: We have a team of dedicated professionals drawn from the maritime as well as IT industry. We specialized in offering ingenious marine software solutions web based or desktop application that are developed specifically for managing and carrying out operations for businesses and organization for their efficient working.
  • Vessel Inspection: Blue Water can arrange professionals from diverse backgrounds which include Superintendents, Technical Managers, Electricians, or exclusive General Inspectors to carry out the inspection.

Our keystone has always been to offer best of consultancy for all your needs, which may regularly consume valuable resources and cause distraction from your core business and strategic planning and assist you to align your organization with the global trends.
