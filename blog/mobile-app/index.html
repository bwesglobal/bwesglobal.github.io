Introducing the BOSS Mobile App

Stay Ahead, Stay Informed.
15 May 2023


We are excited to share that our highly anticipated mobile app for BOSS is now available for download on both iOS and Android smartphones! Now BOSS users can monitor their vessels in real time and stay updated through instant notifications, all from the convenience of their mobile device. The first version of the app comes packed with exciting features such as vessel position and status monitoring, weather visualization and real-time alerts!

With the BOSS mobile app, users can stay on top of their vessel’s ETA, receive alerts for underperformance, track maintenance schedules, and more. This level of real-time visibility will enable users to make informed decisions and respond quickly to any unexpected events that may arise. Additionally, the app will provide access to weather data, route visualization, along with other features that are already available on the BOSS web service.

The new mobile app is a significant step forward for BOSS, as it marks the company’s commitment to continuously improving its services and providing customers with the latest technology. With the mobile app, users can now access BOSS anytime and anywhere, providing greater flexibility and convenience.

“BOSS is committed to providing our customers with the latest technology and services to help them optimize their voyage planning and operations,” said Kumaresh Gupta, Managing Director of Blue Water. “The new mobile app is another step forward in this mission, and we are excited to offer this extended service to our valued customers.”

The BOSS mobile app is available for download on both Apple App Store and Android play store. Users can easily install the app and login using their existing BOSS credentials. Users may also log in using just their email address and a one-time-password (OTP) sent to their email. Once logged in, they will have access to real-time updates and other features already available on the BOSS web service.
